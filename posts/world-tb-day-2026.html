[Organization Updates] [July 16, 2026]

[On this year’s World TB Day, our organization is proud to announce that together we raised $3600 to continue supporting our SF community. We would like to extend our deepest gratitude and appreciation to all the generous donors and everyone who came together to make this a possibility. Your generosity means we can continue to support our SF community and continue to ensure that no one faces TB treatment alone.]

[The funds raised are allocated to our programs: TB Dignity Fund, Food Pantry, Mutual Aid Fund, Advocacy and Community Building, all of which ensure our community always has someone in their corner supporting them along the way while they face TB treatment.]

[World TB day is a reminder of why Together Against TB was founded and its purpose. We believe in the power of uniting as a community to help each other in order to make sure no one faces anything alone. We raise public awareness about tuberculosis and the effects it has in our community. We dedicate ourselves to supporting the mental, physical, social, and financial health of individuals affected by tuberculosis in San Francisco and the Bay Area.]
