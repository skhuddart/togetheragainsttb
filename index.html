No One Faces TB Alone

Together Against TB provides direct support to individuals and families navigating tuberculosis treatment in the San Francisco Bay Area.

Support Our Mission Our Programs

Our Mission

We are dedicated to supporting the mental, physical, social, and financial health of individuals affected by tuberculosis in San Francisco and the Bay Area. Through direct aid, mutual support, and community connection, we ensure that no one faces TB treatment alone.

What We Do

We provide comprehensive support to TB patients through four key programs.

TB Dignity Fund

Essential support packages containing groceries, cleaning supplies, hygiene products, and mobile phones to keep patients connected with their care teams.

Food Pantry

An on-site pantry at the SF TB Clinic stocked with food, hygiene items, clothing, and household essentials for immediate patient access.

Mutual Aid Fund

Emergency support for urgent needs like housing crises, major medical expenses, or family emergencies requiring immediate financial intervention.

Advocacy

Working to improve TB care quality and resources as a member of the Coalition for a TB-Free California.
