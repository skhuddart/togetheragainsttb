Our Programs

We provide comprehensive support to TB patients through four key programs, each designed to address different aspects of the challenges faced during treatment.

TB Dignity Fund

The TB Dignity Fund provides essential support packages to individuals and families navigating TB treatment. These packages are tailored to each person's needs and may include:

  • Groceries and nutritious food
  • Cleaning supplies
  • Hygiene products
  • Household items
  • Clothing
  • Mobile phones with service plans to maintain connection with care teams

To date, we have supported over 50 individuals and their families through this program.

Food Pantry

We maintain an on-site pantry at the San Francisco TB Clinic, ensuring that patients have immediate access to essential items during their clinic visits. The pantry is stocked with:

  • Non-perishable food items
  • Hygiene products
  • Clothing
  • Household essentials

This program removes barriers to accessing basic necessities and provides dignified support when patients need it most.

Mutual Aid Fund

Some situations require more substantial support. Our Mutual Aid Fund responds to urgent, larger needs such as:

  • Emergency housing assistance
  • Major medical expenses not covered by insurance
  • Family crises requiring immediate financial intervention

We have raised over $3,000 specifically for emergency housing and crisis support, helping patients maintain stability during their treatment journey.

Advocacy & Community Building

Beyond direct support, we work to create systemic change. We advocate for resources and policies that improve the quality of TB care for everyone.

Together Against TB is a proud member of the Coalition for a TB-Free California, working alongside other organizations to eliminate TB in our state.

Through our advocacy work, we aim to strengthen connections between patients and clinic staff, improve access to resources, and ensure that the voices of TB patients are heard in policy discussions.
